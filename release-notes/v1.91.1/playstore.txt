What's new in Thor v1.91.1

• New: Extensions store — browse and install verified add-ons right inside Thor. Each download is checked (SHA-256 + signature) before it installs.
• Extensions now open their own settings screen, themed to match Thor (light/dark/AMOLED/dynamic colour).
• Extensions are a power-user feature: the section appears only when you have Root, Shizuku or Dhizuku, with a one-time consent before you enter.
• Two extensions to start: auto-unfreeze suspended apps on launch, and freeze/unfreeze app clusters on a schedule or shortcut.

Thanks for using Thor!
