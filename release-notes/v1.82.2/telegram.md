⚡️ **Thor v1.82.2 is out!**

This major production release rolls up all changes from the **v1.82.x** cycle.

**What's New:**
• **Freezer Overhaul & Safety Gating** (Horizontal floating toolbar, app filters, blocks unsafe freeze & warns on expert packages)
• **Safer System App Disabling & UAD Integration** (pm uninstall/install-existing, safety chips, local icon caching)
• **Portable Installer UI Redesign** (Detailed states, .apkm, .apks, .xapk split APK associations)
• **Adaptive Layout & Navigation Polish** (Navigation rail for tablets, multi-backstack, landscape detail view, backstack crash fixes)
• **UI Preferences & Translations** (Grid/List persistence, Arabic, Spanish, French, and Chinese localization)
• **Critical Crash Fixes** (Gateway errors and button group constraint crash resolutions)
