<p><i>Thor App Manager</i> is an Android App Manager and App Installer utility.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Fully reproducible, copylefted libre software (GPLv3+)</li><li>Material 3 with dynamic colours</li><li>Displays App List while sorting them based on Installation source</li><li>Launch App Activities</li><li>Install/Uninstall Apk files</li><li>Reinstall APKs/app with Google</li><li>Share App Apk file</li><li>Batch Reinstall/Uninstall</li><li>Split App Indicator</li><li>AppState Indicator (frozen = disabled)</li></ul><p>More to come.</p>
